Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing our KaroBooking platform. Please review our refund policy carefully before making a booking:

1. Cancellation by Passenger:
- If you wish to cancel your booking, please do so as soon as possible.
- Refunds for cancelled bookings are subject to the cancellation policy of the respective bus operator.
- Processing fees may apply to cancellations, and refund amounts may vary depending on the timing of the cancellation.

2. Cancellation by Bus Operator:
- In the event of a cancellation by the bus operator due to unforeseen circumstances such as mechanical issues or operational reasons, you will be entitled to a full refund.
- We will notify you of any cancellations by the bus operator and facilitate the refund process accordingly.

3. Refund Processing:
- Refunds will be processed using the original payment method used for the booking.
- Please allow a reasonable processing time for the refund to reflect in your account.
- We are not responsible for any delays in refund processing caused by third-party payment processors or financial institutions.

4. No-show Policy:
- If you fail to board the bus for your scheduled journey without prior cancellation, you will not be eligible for a refund.
- Please ensure that you arrive at the bus stop on time and present valid identification for boarding.

5. Contact Us:
- If you have any questions or concerns about our refund policy or need assistance with a refund request, please contact our customer support team.
- We are here to help you with any refund-related inquiries and ensure a smooth refund process.

By making a booking through our platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to our refund policy. If you have any specific questions about the refund policy for a particular booking or require further clarification, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.

Thank you for choosing us for your bus booking needs. We appreciate your trust in our services.
